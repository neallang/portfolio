Athena's Bump

Description

During my internship, I led the development of a comprehensive mobile application from inception to deployment. The application was focused on providing pregnant women with access and information to nutrition and products during each stage of their pregnancy. This experience significantly enhanced my techincal skills and understanding of full-stack mobile app development. Beyond writing code, I learned a lot about the nuances involved in planning and developing a project of this scale for a real company. I was involved in all stages of the project lifecycle, from initial concept and requirements to testing and optimization. My contributions helped the company move closer to its goal of creating a high-quality, market-ready mobile application, showcasing my ability to deliver impactful results.

Features

Google Login: Implemented a secure Google authentication, enhancing user convenience.

App Store Readiness: Prepared the application for deployment to the App Store through testing, debugging, and optimization.
