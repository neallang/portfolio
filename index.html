About Me

Hello! I'm Neal, a fourth-year student studying computer science at the University of Virginia. My coding journey began when I took 'Introduction to Programming' at UVA. While this was the hardest course I'd taken at the time, I found the assignments extremely thought-provoking and rewarding. There's nothing like the feeling of figuring out a solution or bug after hours of grinding, and this exact feeling inspired me to become a TA for the course the following semester. I now also lead a weekly lab of students and other TAs with the hopes of helping students reach that 'Aha!' moment.

Since then, I've seen success in programming both within and beyond the classroom. I've been able to maintain a strong GPA while balancing difficult course loads. Alongside my role as a TA, I also had the opportunity to teach courses in Python and Java last summer. More recently, I've found a passion for web development, and I've used my learned skills to help several companies develop their websites and a mobile application. As I enjoy full-stack development, my objective is to help solve problems and ultimately bridge the gap between people and technology through user-friendly interfaces and experiences.

If you're looking for a hard worker with strong communication skills to add to your team, or you're just looking for an extra set of eyes and hands regarding a project, please reach out. Let's connect and pursue exciting new opportunities!
