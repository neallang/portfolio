About Me

Hello! I'm Neal, a fourth-year student studying computer science at the University of Virginia. My coding journey began when I took 'Introduction to Programming' at UVA. While challenging, I found the assignments extremely thought-provoking and rewarding. There's nothing like the feeling of figuring out a solution or bug after hours of grinding, and this exact feeling inspired me to become a TA for the course the following semester. I now also lead a weekly lab of students and other TAs with the hopes of helping students reach that 'Aha!' moment.

Alongside my role as a TA, I also had the opportunity to teach courses in Python and Java last summer. More recently, I've found a passion for full-stack development, and I've used my learned skills to help several companies develop their websites and a mobile application. My objective is to creatively solve problems and ultimately bridge the gap between people and technology through user-friendly interfaces and experiences.

If you're looking for a hard worker with strong communication skills to add to your team, or you're just looking for an extra set of eyes and hands regarding a project, please reach out. Let's connect and pursue exciting new opportunities!
